Overview
COURSE DESCRIPTION

This college offers a Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering. This course falls under the Engineering and Technology programme. This course is affiliated under Uttar Pradesh Board of Technical Education, Lucknow. This course is offered in 1st shift and is a Full Time diploma course. The maximum allowed student intake for Computer Science and Engineering diploma course is 60 for the academic year 2019-2020.

LEARNING OUTCOMES
  • Knowledge of fundamental computer concepts and principles : This includes topics such as data structures, algorithms, programming languages, operating systems, and computer networks.
  • Ability to design, implement, and evaluate computer-based solutions : This includes the ability to identify and define problems, develop solutions, and test and evaluate the results.
  • Communication skills : This includes the ability to communicate technical concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Problem-solving skills : This includes the ability to identify and solve problems using a variety of techniques.
  • Teamwork skills : This includes the ability to work effectively as part of a team to achieve common goals.
  • Professionalismc : This includes the ability to adhere to ethical standards and professional conduct.
